Why should you work with us?

Sunset Airlines is an equal opportunity employer who needs hardworking indiviuals like you. Sunset airlines provides benefits such as medical coverage, a 401(k) plan, and cah rewards with our wellness programs. On top of all of these benefits, Sunset Airlines will also provide all of your training not just for free, but you will get paid to go through it. Lastly, you will be working at an airline so of course you will get flights for absolutely free! As long as there is remaining space for you.

How to apply

Applying for Sunset Airlines is easy. Start by sending an email to careers@sunsetairlines.com providing your name, phone number, resume, birthdate, and what job you are applying for of course. This email will serve as your cover letter. After submitting your application you will recieve an email regarding your decision within two weeks. Details for what to do next will be in the email.
